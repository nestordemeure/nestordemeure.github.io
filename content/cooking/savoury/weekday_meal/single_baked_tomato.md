This is a great way to add vegetables to a meal: it takes seconds to prepare and can then bake in the oven while you focus on the rest of the meal.
Plus, it only requires ingredients you always have in your pantry.

## Ingredients:

* 1 big tomato[^tomato]
* olive oil
* a pinch of salt

[^tomato]: Since the tomato is pretty much the only ingredient, you want it ripe and in season.


## Cooking:

* rub the tomato in olive oil
* place it in a ramekin[^ramekin]
* cover it with a *generous*[^salt] sprinkle of salt
* cook in the oven until the tomato is burning hot to the touch

[^ramekin]: Or a small bowl, many things will do. It is there to capture any juice that may leak out during cooking.

[^salt]: Generous because it is the only seasoning for the full tomato.
