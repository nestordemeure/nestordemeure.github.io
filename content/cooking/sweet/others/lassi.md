Lassi is *great*. It is refreshing, rich in fruits and protein, delicious, yet quick and easy to make.

## Ingredients (1 tall glass):

* 2/3[^fraction] unsweetened whole-fat yogurt[^greek]
* 1/3 fruits[^fruits]
* 1.5 teaspoons white sugar (to taste)

[^fraction]: Yes, we are measuring in fractions of your glass.

[^greek]: Yogurt being the main ingredient, you want a good *fat* one. Greek yogurt is ideal. You can also use a coconut yogurt for a vegan alternative.

[^fruits]: Mango is a classic (whether fresh or frozen/canned pulp), but you can use pretty much anything: cantaloupe, blueberries, strawberries, etc. I do like to use some frozen fruits to get a chilly end result without having to dilute it by adding ice to the blender.

## Preparation:

* Put the ingredients in a blender[^cleaning]
* Blend until the fruits are incorporated[^liquid]
* Serve still cold, in a glass with a straw.

[^cleaning]: The recipe is nice in that a quick rinse is enough to clean all of the material used.

[^liquid]: The end result gets more liquid as you blend it. Since I like my lassi thick, I blend it as little as I can, but you might want to blend it thoroughly (you can also add whole-fat milk if you want it even more liquid).
